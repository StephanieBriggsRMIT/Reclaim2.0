The Reclaim Application

The Reclaim app encourages users to take responsibility for their contributions to the environment, allowing them to keep track of their weekly groceries and explore options in reducing food scraps and wastage with the items they have. Reclaims main feature is a community-based platform that connects users to their likeminded neighbours who share the same goals, to encourage the growth of sustainable practices and to support each other in making the right decisions for our future. Food items, resources and friendly tips are just a few of the things that can be shared amongst the community!
